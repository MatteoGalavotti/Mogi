About Mogi

Mogi is a free, open-source and responsive HTML & CSS Framework. You just have to integrate the style, get the components and you're ready to go! There are 2 principal styles, dark and light, you can use just one or you can even mix them together depending on your own preferences. Mogi was born in the July of 2019 when his creator decided to make an easy to use and light framework. Only pure Html and Css are used.
